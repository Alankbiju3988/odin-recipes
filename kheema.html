Masala Kheema - Dry Spicy Minced Meat

This simple meat dish comes together in
as little as 35 minutes using any ground meat you prefer.
Chicken, lamb, pork, goat, and beef all work well.
A flavorful blend of garlic, onion, ginger, and spices
like garam masala all give it a lovely fragrance and a little spice.
Serve it with naan, other flatbread, or white rice as a main dish.
