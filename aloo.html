Baingan Aloo Ki Subji (Eggplant and Potatoes)

Eggplant, potatoes, and tomatoes make up Baingan aloo ki subji,
a flavorful vegetable curry that vegetarians and vegans can enjoy.
Like many Indian dishes, the blend of spices tastes even better a
day or so afterward so the flavors have time to get to know each
other. Pair it with hot chapatis and cool raita and chutney.
